Your questions answered.

Common Issues

  • Unable to Cast a Vote: Ensure you are registered as a voter. If you are, check your voter ID and try again.
  • Forgot My Voter ID: Contact the admin or election authority to retrieve your voter ID.
  • Cannot See Results: Ensure the election has concluded. Results are only displayed after voting is complete.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is blockchain voting?
    Blockchain voting is a secure and transparent way to conduct elections using blockchain technology, ensuring votes are immutable and verifiable.
  • How is my vote secure?
    Each vote is encrypted and stored immutably on the blockchain, preventing tampering.
  • How do I register as a voter?
    Navigate to the Voter Section and use the "Register Voter" form to register with your unique voter ID.
  • Can I change my vote after casting it?
    No, votes are final once cast to maintain the integrity of the election process.
